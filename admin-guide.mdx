### Admin Dashboard Features

This guide is broken down into sections based on the different tabs available in the Admin Dashboard:

- [**Management**](https://www.google.com/search?q=./management) Your central hub for creating and managing users, courses, and certification paths.
- [**Certification Matrix**](https://www.google.com/search?q=./certification-matrix) A powerful dashboard for tracking company-wide compliance and user progress.
- [**Usage Statistics**](https://www.google.com/search?q=./usage-statistics) A data-driven look at user engagement and platform activity.
- [**AI Question Generator**](https://www.google.com/search?q=./question-generator) A tool to automatically create course content from PDF documents.
- [**Feedback Customization**](https://www.google.com/search?q=./feedback-customization) A section to personalize the feedback users receive during quizzes.
